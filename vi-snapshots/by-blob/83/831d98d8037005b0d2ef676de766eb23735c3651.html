Controller.lvlib:Controller.lvclass:Stop Core.vi

<p>(<strong>Filename</strong>: <span class="dnl">Actor Framework.lvlib:Actor.lvclass:Stop Core.vi</span>)</p>

<p>Defines what the actor does before it stops. Use the <strong>final error code</strong> input to determine whether the actor shut down in response to an error.</p>

<p>By default, this method passes the Stop message along to all <a href="nihelplauncher://docs/csh?context=lvcore_lvcomm_af_launch_nested_actor">auto-stop nested actors</a>. A descendant class may override this method to define additional behavior, such as shutting down any processes the actor initiated in its override of the <a href="nihelplauncher://docs/csh?context=lvcore_lvcomm_af_actor_core">Actor Core</a> method.</p>
