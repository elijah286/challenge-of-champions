MC Prompt.lvlib:MC Prompt.lvclass:Actor Core.vi

<p>(<strong>Filename</strong>: <span class="dnl">Actor Framework.lvlib:Actor.lvclass:Actor Core.vi</span>)</p>

<p>Receives messages to the actor, reacts to them, and initiates any error handling that may shut the actor down.</p>

<p>Descendant classes may override this VI to append parallel tasks for the actor to complete while it handles messages. Overrides use the <a href="nihelplauncher://docs/csh?context=lvcore_glang_udclass">Call Parent Class Method</a> node.</p>
